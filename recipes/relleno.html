Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Italian Herbs

Method:

  1. Boil Pasta for up to 4 minutes or to preference.
  2. Mash tomatoes and saute until melted.
  3. Add Pasta to tomato sauce.
  4. Add salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and italian herbs to taste.
