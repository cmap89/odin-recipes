Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (3-4 lbs.)
  • 2-3 tsp. Grace white vinegar
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 10 spice seeds
  • 2 jalape√±o peppers or banana peppers
  • 3 lbs. sliced white onions
  • 10 oregano leaves
  • 1 head garlic

Method:

  1. Boil spices in water, add chicken and cool until tender.
  2. Remove chicken from broth and put the broth aside for later.
  3. Cut chicken into parts and season thoroughly.
  4. PLace chicken parts on a baking pan and bake at 400 degrees until golden brown.
  5. Add vinegar to the broth (to taste) along with boiled garlic and peppers.
  6. Pour boiling water over onions to remove rinds.
  7. Add sliced onions to broth and cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. Remove soup from heat.
  9. Serve hot with fresh corn tortillas.
