Belizean Rice And Beans

Description

As a Belizean staple, this dish is the perfect accompaniment to any main course from the classic stewed chicken to the freshly caught fried fish. Made with a fresh selection of ingredients, coconut rice and beans is simple to make and guaranteed to please!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red kidney beans
  • 1 onion sliced
  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/4 lb. salted meat (cut into small pieces)
  • 1 cup thick coconut cream
  • 1 clove garlic (optional)
  • salt and pepper to taste

Method:

  1. Soak beans overnight in enough water to cover fully.
  2. Boil beans until tender but still whole, adding salted meat and garlic when almost tender.
  3. Add coconut cream, onion and seasonings.
  4. Wash rice and add to beans.
  5. Cook over gentle heat until liquid is absorbed.
  6. Stir gently with a fork, and add a little water from time to time, until rice is fully cooked.
  7. Serve hot with your choice of protein.
